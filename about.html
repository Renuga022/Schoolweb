About

Teaching Approach

We believe a truly international school not only has international teachers, students, curriculum in a world-class facility, but it also embeds an international perspective that embraces a global community.

Superintendent's Message

We believe the true nature of education is a collaboration between child, parent, school and community. This is even more poignant in today’s fast-paced and technology focused world.

Be Part of Our Community

We openly welcome you to come and see for yourself the wonderful family atmosphere and attention to detail that will allow us to ensure your child meets their full potential. Please feel free to attend any of our open days or make an appointment for a personal tour of our school.

Curriculum

A student-centered, holistic and inquiry based learning approach challenges our students to construct a deeper meaning and understanding of concepts. Skills and strategies are learned through systematic instructions and applied in engaging project work, investigations and authentic activities with a blend of child guided and teacher guided learning.
