ACADAMICS

June customises a personal educational journey for every student based on their strengths and passions.

Welcome to June School

To provide an outstanding international educational experience, that enables students to develop into creative global citizens and future leaders.

To ensure every child meets their full potential by developing their intellect, character, and well-being through community collaboration in an learning environment.

  • Our Campus

    Our purpose-designed building has been thoughtfully created with young learners in mind, providing an environment that truly supports each child's learning.

    Our outdoor playground is designed to allow children to enjoy their childhood and engage in unstructured play in a natural environment. The playground and swimming pool helps children develop a love for being physically active.

  • Our Facilities

    Classrooms are all connected to a large central atelier space, designed to promote the various learning objectives for early childhood through an exploratory approach.

    The facility offers a multitude of teaching-learning opportunities in well planned and spacious classrooms.
